Micro Mayhem is an educational adventure where students explore the human immune system through the power of Boolean logic. Navigate microscopic worlds, wire together logic circuits, and protect the body from a mysterious threat.

Grades 5 – 8  /  Ages 11 – 14
01

Explore the Microscopic World

Dive into cells, tissues, and the immune system. Discover how the body defends itself in a vibrant, scientifically-grounded world.

02

Master Boolean Logic

Learn AND, OR, and NOT gates through hands-on puzzles. Build computational thinking by wiring logic circuits to solve real challenges.

03

Save the Cell!

Guide Byte the robot through an action-packed adventure. Outsmart viruses, recruit immune allies, and protect the body.

A World Inside
the Body

Journey through breathtaking landscapes hidden within the human body — from crimson valleys of the bloodstream to towering cellular fortresses.

Something ancient stirs at the heart of the immune system. A mysterious energy pyramid has appeared, disrupting the delicate balance. Only Byte can reach it. Only you can solve the puzzles that stand in the way.

Meet the Cast

Dr. Maya

Scientist & Guide

A brilliant immunologist who shrinks you down to explore the microscopic world. She guides your journey with wisdom, curiosity, and the occasional dad joke.

The Professor

Mad Scientist & Antagonist

A wild-haired eccentric who weaponizes Boolean logic against the immune system. Brilliant, unpredictable, and always one step ahead.

Byte

Robot Companion

Your trusty green robot sidekick. Byte interfaces with logic gates, scans for pathogens, and speaks exclusively in binary puns. Small but mighty.
