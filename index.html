A World Inside
the Body

Shrink and grow between layers of the body — from crimson valleys of the bloodstream to towering cellular fortresses to the deepest reaches of the immune system.

Something mysterious is happening to the immune system. A mysterious new case has arrived, and the body's defenses are failing. Only you can shrink down, explore the layers of the body, and solve the puzzles to save it.
