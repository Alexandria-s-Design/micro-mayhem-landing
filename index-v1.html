About the Game

Learn Immunity. Master Logic. Save the Cell.

Micro Mayhem is an educational adventure game where students explore the human immune system through the power of Boolean logic. Navigate microscopic worlds, solve logic-gate puzzles, and battle invaders threatening the body's defenses.

Designed for Grades 5–8 • Ages 11–14

Explore the Microscopic World

Dive deep into cells, tissues, and the immune system. Discover how the body defends itself from pathogens in a vibrant, scientifically-grounded world.

Master Boolean Logic

Learn AND, OR, and NOT gates through hands-on puzzles. Build computational thinking skills by wiring together logic circuits to solve real challenges.

Save the Cell!

Guide Byte the robot through an action-packed adventure. Outsmart viruses, recruit immune allies, and protect the body from a mysterious threat.

Meet the Team

Your Guides Through the Micro World

Dr. Maya

Scientist & Guide

A brilliant immunologist who shrinks you down to explore the microscopic world. She provides scientific context and guides your journey with wisdom, curiosity, and the occasional dad joke.

The Professor

Mad Scientist & Antagonist

A wild-haired eccentric who has discovered how to weaponize Boolean logic against the immune system. His chaotic experiments create the challenges you must overcome. Brilliant, unpredictable, and always one step ahead.

Byte

Robot Companion & Mascot

Your trusty green robot sidekick who speaks in binary puns. Byte can interface with logic gates, scan for pathogens, and fits in the palm of a white blood cell. Small but mighty!

Game World

A World Inside the Body

Journey through breathtaking landscapes hidden within the human body. From the crimson valleys of the bloodstream to the towering structures of cellular fortresses, every environment is a scientifically-inspired adventure waiting to be explored.

Something ancient stirs at the heart of the immune system. A mysterious energy pyramid has appeared deep within the body's defenses, disrupting the delicate balance that keeps us healthy. Only Byte can reach it. Only you can solve the logic puzzles that stand in the way.
